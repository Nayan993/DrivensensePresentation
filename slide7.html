Control Scheme

GESTURE CONTROLS

INPUT SOURCE: WEBCAM

STEERING

AXIS X

Open Index Finger or Pinky Finger Left or Right to steer the car. Mimics a virtual steering wheel.

SENSITIVITY: 85%

ACCELERATION

AXIS Y+

Open Your Palm Forward to accelerate. Distance determines speed.

THROTTLE: LINEAR

BRAKE / REVERSE

AXIS Y-

Close Your Fist, Two Fingers to apply brakes or reverse.

RESPONSE: INSTANT

AUTO PAUSE

SAFETY

If the hand is Lost or exits the camera frame, the speed automatically slows.

FAILSAFE ACTIVE
