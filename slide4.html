Project Background

INSPIRATION &
MOTIVATION

REAL-WORLD INTUITION

Mimicking natural driving habits. The goal is to translate physical steering gestures into a digital environment without the need for learning complex key binds.

ACCESSIBILITY & INCLUSIVITY

Gaming should be for everyone. By removing the need for expensive steering wheels or controllers, we lower the barrier to entry for immersive racing.

PUSHING TECH BOUNDARIES

Exploring the limits of Computer Vision in real-time gaming. transforming a simple webcam into a high-latency input device.
